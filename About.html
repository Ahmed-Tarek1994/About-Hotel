About this hotel

A 10-minute walk from Gare du Nord train station, this low-key hostel in the Montmartre district is surrounded by bars and restaurants. It's also an 8-minute walk from Sacré-Cœur basilica and a 13-minute walk from the Moulin Rouge.

Colorful mixed dorms with bunk beds sleep up to 10 people and offer shared bathrooms; some add views of the Sacré-Cœur. Private rooms are available, some with en suite bathrooms, while others have shared facilities.

Continental breakfast is complimentary. The reception area offers free WiFi and shared computers. There's also a communal kitchen, a lounge with a foosball table, and laundry facilities.
